About

Hi, I’m Ben Love, a freelance handyman with a passion for helping people maintain and improve their homes and houseboats. After spending years in construction and learning the ins and outs of everything from plumbing to electrical work, I decided to bring my skills to the freelance world. I’ve built my business on trust, quality, and the satisfaction of my clients.

House Services

  • Plumbing Repairs: Leaky pipes, faucet installations, and more.
  • Electrical Work: Light fixture installations, wiring, and electrical troubleshooting.
  • Home Renovations: Kitchen remodeling, bathroom updates, drywall repairs.
  • General Maintenance: Painting, furniture assembly, odd jobs.

Houseboat Services

  • Marine Plumbing & Electrical: Repairs and upgrades for houseboat systems.
  • Deck & Exterior Maintenance: Waterproofing, painting, and cleaning.
  • Custom Installations: Build and install custom storage, furniture, or systems tailored to small houseboat spaces.
